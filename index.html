Who Are We?

The Hilltop Council is Hilltop Apartment's governing body of student's dedicated to promoting social activities, community outreach, and improvement of the University. It serves to engender a deeper appreciation and respect for fellow members of the community, promote personal pride and responsibility for living in Hilltop Apartments, and encourage intellectual, physical, emotional, and social development.

What do we do?

Being a part of Hilltop Council gives you the opportunity to experience a real world leadership first hand. Due to the nature of things, living in Hilltop causes many people to feel secluded, and council tries to overcome that by planning events that bring together everyone in the community.

Get Involved!


Biweekly Meetings

Meetings are held every other Tuesday at 8PM in Owen Hall's recreational room.

Leadership Position

Becoming a senator for your building puts you in charge of making sure your community's voice is heard at meetings.
